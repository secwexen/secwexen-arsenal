# Ethics & Responsible Use Policy

## Purpose

The tools and code in this repository are developed **strictly for educational, research, and authorized security purposes**.  
This ethics document clarifies acceptable usage and responsibilities when working with this toolkit.

## Ethical Principles

### 1. **Educational & Authorized Use Only**

- Tools in this collection are intended to be used in **controlled lab environments**, **authorized penetration tests**, or **research settings**.
- Do **not** deploy them against systems or networks that you do not own or do not have explicit written permission to test.

### 2. **Legal Compliance**

- All use must comply with applicable laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.
- The author **does not endorse, condone, or support illegal activity** or malicious misuse.

### 3. **Respect for Privacy and Data**

- Never attempt to access, monitor, or manipulate data belonging to others without proper authorization.
- Avoid actions that could lead to unauthorized data exposure or privacy violations.

### 4. **Responsible Disclosure**

- If you discover vulnerabilities during authorized testing, follow responsible disclosure practices and notify affected organizations promptly.

### 5. **Safety and Containment**

- Always operate tools in isolated or test environments when possible.
- Do not expose production systems or live networks to unmanaged risk.
